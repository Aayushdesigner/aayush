About School

Gorkha Higher Secondary School, Tulsipur serves over 2500 students. It offers +2 programs in Science and Management stream affiliated to HSEB. It aims to prepare youths creative and productive enough to tackle the growing challenges of the current business and techno world. Regarding the +2 programs and high demand of parents, the school is going to offer two shifts: Day shift and Morning shift in both Science and Management streams.Gorkha Higher Secondary School, one of the reputed and emerging private schools in Nepal, was established in 2011 by Gorkha Education Group to provide quality education for current and future generation of students. It is located at the sound and peaceful panorama of Tulsipur-6, Baruwagaun, Dang covering an area of 3 bigha Land. Gorkha Higher Secondary School has involved Ex-Gurkhas (Lahures), serving armies and local intellectual personalities. The Gorkha Education Group has been running Secondary Schools, Gorkha International Public Higher Secondary School, Ghorahi, Gorkha Higher Secondary School, Tulsipur and Gorkha Model Secondary School, Lamahi, Bachelor College, Victoria International College, Ghorahi, Gorkha Model College, Tulsipur and Swornim College, Lamahi and Gorkha Hospital, Ghorahi.

Gorkha Higher Secondary School, Tulsipur has now become the chosen learning-center for students. Experienced and qualified faculty members, high discipline and dedication to scholarship with a well equipped Physics, Chemistry and Biology Lab, Computer lab, Library, interactive classroom, the state of art teaching methods, educational excursions etc. have definitely fascinated the students to choose this school for study.

Student’s personal care, counseling and guidance are the topmost priorities of the school from the very day. As a result, the performance and the achievement of the students have made Gorkha Higher Secondary School a leading institution in the country.

About Computer Education

Computer education is acquiring basic knowledge and skills of how to operate computers to perform appropriate jobs. It is a field of study that involves branches of study in various sectors apart from computer knowledge. Computer Education plays a very integral and fundamental role in getting jobs these days. What are the benefits of learning computer education? Many students might be interested in knowing the significance of computer education. Let us discuss more about what is computer education and its benefits. Helps in getting righteous jobs: Nowadays the computer industry is growing at a very rapid rate. Computers have become an integral part of the professional sector. Students must gain a good command of the field of computers. The remuneration package offered to those who have good knowledge of computers is higher than any other person who is not acquainted with computer education. Increases efficiency of a person: A computer is an electronic device that can store, retrieve, and process data. You may already know that a computer can be used to maintain records, type documents, send emails, create presentations, and much more. A computer simply makes any task very easy to perform and therefore leads to overall growth in the performance of a person. Makes communication easy: In today’s world, we can use computers for communication. It provides us with the facilities like chatting, calling, video conferencing, etc. To use these facilities one must have a command of computer education.
